Jahangirnagar University maintains a central cafeteria for all kind of usage. It is behind the monument called 
“Omor Ekushey”. There are always students in the cafeteria. The cafeteria offers the students and teachers a healthy 
and fresh meal in morning and lunch time in very low cost. There are 29 staffs and cookers helping to serve the food 
and maintaining the menus.

Some Basic Information to book Cafeteria

There are some formalities to hire the cafeteria but this must be the time when the cafeteria is close (i.e. it is after
 the daily schedule or off days and holidays). Teachers and students also can arrange any program at very low cost. 
The charges of hiring the cafe as follows:

Any relative of any teacher………………………………………..4000/-
Anyone referred. by any teacher…………………………………6000/-
Any program of any department……………………………………500/-
The bill must be paid through the bank. There are also some form fill ups to fulfill the formalities.
