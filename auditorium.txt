
The "Zahir Raihan auditorium", located in the TSC complex, is named after Zahir Raihan, a famous Bangladeshi novelist,
 writer and filmmaker. There is also a seminar hall for seminars, meetings and cultural programmes. The auditorium has
 1500 seats, while the seminar hall can accommodate 250 persons. It is an auditorium fully equipped for drama, cultural 
activities, international conferences, and other similar events.
