Jahangirnagar University was formally launched on 12 January 1970 under the Jahangirnagar Muslim University Ordinance,
1970 and this day is observed as University Day. Initially, it was named Jahangirnagar Muslim University, and the plan
was to operate the university like Aligarh Muslim University. But after the independence of Bangladesh, its name changed
to Jahangirnagar University under the Jahangirnagar University Act' 1973.
